Vera

Act4

PRINCE PETRO. My dear General, your nephew must be a perfect Turk. He seems to get married three times a week regularly. GEN. KOT. Well, he wants a dowry to console him. COUNT R. I am sick of town. I want a house in the country. MARQ. DE POIV. I am sick of the country. I want a house in town. BARON RAFF. Mes amis, I am extremely sorry for you. It is out of the question. PRINCE PETRO. But my son, Baron? GEN. KOTEMK. But my nephew? MARQ. DE POIV. But my house in town? COUNT R. But my house in the country? MARQ. DE POIV. But my wife's diamond bracelet?
